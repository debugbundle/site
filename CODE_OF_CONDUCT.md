# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical outcomes and collaboration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
